Rabanada: Brazilian French Toast

| Serves: 4-5 | Course: Breakfast | Prep Time: 10 mins | Cook Time: 20 mins | Total Time: 30 mins | Cuisine: Brazilian |

  • On a large plate, mix the sugar and cinnamon until blended, set aside.
  • Place the milk in one shallow bowl and the eggs in another. Lightly beat the eggs.
  • In a medium skillet, heat Â½ inch of oil to just before it begins to smoke (350-375F).
  • Working with a few slices at a time, dip the bread in the milk letting it soak in.
  • Dip the bread in the beaten egg, coating both sides.
  • Place soaked bread in hot oil and fry for 2-3 mins on each side, or until golden brown.
  • Carefully remove the fried French toast from hot oil and place on a platter lined with paper towels.
  • Continue with the remaining bread.
  • While the fried French toast is still warm, dip both sides in the plate with cinnamon sugar, and serve.
